At VILLAGE, we believe in learning through curiosity. Now is a magical time to join. We're still small, and every new hire makes a massive difference by taking on ownership of this mission. We’re looking for a UX designer to help us grow! This is a full-time, exempt* position that reports to the Director of Product. Working at VILLAGE:

  • Join a small team (currently there are 7 of us) of passionate problem solvers.
  • Full-time remote role, and/or on-site in our Eau Claire, WI office.
  • Market competitive salary with early-stage stock options.
  • Flexible vacation policy with paid time off.
  • Significant career growth opportunities.
  • A fun culture, including things like team events, happy hours, and trips.

*An exempt employee is an employee who does not receive overtime pay or qualify for minimum wage.
